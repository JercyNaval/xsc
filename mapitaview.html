MAPITA VIEW DECK

Mapita View Deck is located at Sitio Mapita in Barangay Laoag. The Mapita View Deck is a place where you can see a wide and beautiful view of the surroundings. It is like standing on a high spot and looking out to see all the land and maybe even some mountains or water from far away. People go there to relax, take pictures, and enjoy the peaceful feeling of being close to nature. .
